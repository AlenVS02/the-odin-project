Chicken Parmesan Pasta Casserole

Description

This easy canned chicken casserole is a delicious alternative to homemade chicken Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rotini pasta
  • 1 (12 ounce) can chicken chunks, drained
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Â½ cup seasoned bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat. Cook rotini in boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Stir together cooked rotini, chicken, and mozzarella cheese in a large casserole dish. Pour marinara sauce over pasta mixture; sprinkle with bread crumbs. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 35 minutes.
